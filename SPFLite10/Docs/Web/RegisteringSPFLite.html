Registering SPFLite


A small donation will register you for nag-free SPFLite.   If you register, you will receive an eMail containing the instructions and a small tool to remove the trial status of your copy.



My preferred payment method is via PayPal, which is usually the simplest form for most people.


You can also register using a normal personal cheque  via ordinary mail.


Note: Do not use a USPS Money order - they are negotiable only within the US and it's territories.  

I can't cash them (I'm in Canada) and it's ridiculously difficult for you to get your money back from the USPS.
